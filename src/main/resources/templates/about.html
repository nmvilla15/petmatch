Our Team

Sarah Jordan

Founder

As a Pre-Veterinary student working at an animal clinic Sarah saw a need to have a quick, streamlined process for adoptions and sought out a better way to achieve this goal. Woofer combines the same logic you see on other pet adoption sites and combines it with the easy "swipe" feature you would find on dating services to create a product that aims to clear out shelters/rescues at a much quicker rate.
